验证文件的 MD5/SHA1/SHA256

Posted on 2018-06-29 Edited on 2018-07-01 Disqus:

验证SHA-256
$ openssl dgst -sha256 /path/to/file

验证SHA-1
$ openssl sha1 /path/to/file

验证 MD5
$ openssl md5 /path/to/file
